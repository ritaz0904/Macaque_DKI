Macaque_DKI

The dataset contains multi-shell raw diffusion MRI data and fitted mean kurtosis(MK) map of an ex-vivo healthy macaque brain. This dataset was acquired by the group at Department of Radiology, Children’s Hospital of Philadelphia, Perelman School of Medicine, University of Pennsylvania, directed by Dr. Hao Huang (hao.huang@pennmedicine.upenn.edu).

Dataset maintainer:Tianjia Zhu (tianjiazhu6@gmail.com)

This dataset was used in the study "Neuroanatomical underpinning of diffusion kurtosis measurements in the cerebral cortex of healthy macaque brains" (in revision, Magnetic Resonance in Medicine) available at https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2020.07.25.221093v2.abstract. This manuscipt should be cited appropriately for using the dataset.
